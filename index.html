How to Use Foodie's Travel Vault

Are you serious about food and traveling?

Are you overwhelmed with dining options when on a trip?

Then this app is for you!

As you research the best restaurants you want to vist when traveling in the United States, simply add them to your vault by selecting the location and the restaurant name. You can then add a date that works best with your itinerary.

Changed your mind about the date or restaurant?

Click on the date to change it and click "Add Date" to save.

Click on to remove the restaurant from the vault.
